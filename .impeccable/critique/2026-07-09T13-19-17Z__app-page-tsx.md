## Priority Issues

1. **[P3] Hover grammar is uneven** (user-raised; confirmed in browser). The featured cards are the benchmark (lift + shadow + pink title + arrow slide), but: nav links and filter tabs respond with only a faint text-lighten; the FilterSelect trigger and the expandable timeline rows have NO hover response at all; the nav brand has none; the pink CTAs move (2px lift) but don't change color, which reads as static in practice. Fix: one quiet grammar — surface-tint on hover for pill-shaped controls (nav links, tabs, dropdown trigger), a text/chevron lighten on timeline rows, and a slight brightness shift on the pink CTAs alongside the lift. All tone shifts, no new pink. **Command**: /impeccable polish.
2. **[P3] Buttons wrap 3+1 in the docked card on short viewports** (s < 1 makes the 8em basis fit three across). Tidy but not the designed 2×2. Cosmetic; only on <860px-tall screens.
